SmartSolar MPPT 150/35

The SmartSolar MPPT 150/35 is a solar charge controller. A solar charge controller is used to gather energy from solar panels, and after that, the controller stores the gathered energy in your batteries. The SmartSolar charge controller maximises the energy-harvest. The charge controller also maintains battery health. The controller uses the modern MPPT technology. Another feature of the charge controller is remote monitoring and control. With Bluetooth you can pair your smartphone or other device with the charge controller. Click here to order the product.

Multiplus

The Multplus is a combined inverter rand charger in one beautiful package. It has a lot of features including a true sine wave inverter, adaptive charging, hybrid PowerAssist technology, plus multiple system integration features. The PowerAssist feature prevents an overload of the limited AC source. The MultiPlus also has virtually unlimited power thanks to parallel operation. There is also an option on the MultiPlus to monitor and control the system remotely via the Internet. This means you can control it wherever you are. Click here to order the product.
